Recommendations

Try to link each secondment to 1–3 skills, depending on its duration. Do not select too many skills for a single short secondment. PhD students should focus on developing a few key skills rather than spreading themselves too thin across many areas, and each secondment should last at least 3 months. It is valuable to link a secondment to more than one WP in order to ensure interdisciplinarity. Do not be too ambitious in the number of skills selected or in the scope of the description; all secondments should be feasible within the planned timeframe.
